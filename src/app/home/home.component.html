COVID-19 is an illness caused by a coronavirus that can spread from person to person. The virus was first detected in late 2019 in Wuhan, China and has since spread globally.

Maintain two or more meters distance between yourself and anybody else, especially if they show symptoms. Do not shake hands, hug or touch anybody. Avoid large gatherings of people, including sporting events, religious gatherings, parties, etc. Find more guidelines on physical distancing

Clean your hands with hand sanitizer when soap and water are not available. Hand sanitizer is less effective when your hands are visibility dirty. If they are, then you should wash with soap and water.
